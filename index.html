🚫 Mistakes to Avoid — Can Cost You Lakhs

❌ Hiding medical history
If dad's past illness or smoking is not declared and a related claim comes up, the insurer WILL reject it entirely. Full disclosure is non-negotiable.
❌ Buying the cheapest plan with co-pay
30% co-pay on a ₹5 lakh surgery = ₹1.5 lakh from YOUR pocket. Zero co-pay plans are worth the extra premium.
❌ "We're healthy, we don't need insurance"
Heart surgery: ₹3–8L. Knee replacement: ₹2–4L. Cancer: ₹10–20L. One hospitalisation can wipe out years of savings.
❌ Waiting to buy later
Premium rises sharply with age. Every year you delay = higher premium + longer wait for pre-existing coverage.
❌ Not checking network hospitals near your home
If the nearest network hospital is 50 km away, cashless claims become impractical. Check BEFORE buying.

📖 Insurance Terms — Simple Explanations

Share with parents so they understand the policy. Telugu hints included.

Sum Insured
Maximum amount insurer will pay. ₹15L SI = up to ₹15L covered per year. (గరిష్ట కవరేజ్ మొత్తం)
Premium
Yearly fee to keep insurance active. Like a subscription. (ప్రతి సంవత్సరం చెల్లించే మొత్తం)
Co-pay
% YOU pay from every claim. 20% co-pay on ₹5L bill = ₹1L from your pocket. This plan = ZERO co-pay. (మీ జేబు నుండి వెళ్ళే భాగం)
PED Waiting Period
Already-existing conditions won't be covered for 2–4 years. (ముందే ఉన్న వ్యాధులకు 2-4 సంవత్సరాలు ఆగాలి)
Cashless Claim
Hospital bills insurer directly — you pay nothing. Only at network hospitals. (హాస్పిటల్ డైరెక్ట్‌గా కంపెనీకి బిల్ చేస్తుంది)
Room Rent Cap
Some plans cap room type. This plan = NO cap, any room.
Restore Benefit
Cover refills after a claim for subsequent claims in the same year. (క్లెయిమ్ తర్వాత కవరేజ్ రీఫిల్ అవుతుంది)
Solvency Ratio
Can the company pay when you claim? IRDAI minimum = 1.50. Higher = safer.
Deductible
Amount you pay first before insurance kicks in. ₹50K deductible = you pay first ₹50K, insurer pays the rest. Higher deductible = lower premium.
